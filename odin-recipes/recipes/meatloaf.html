Meatloaf

Worlds best meatloaf recipe!

Making meatloaf can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step by step instructions in teh recipe below, but lets go over the basics.

It tastes real good too I tell you what!!!!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 small onion chopped
  • 3/4 cup of bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Mix beef, onion, bread crumbs, and egg in a bowl.
  3. Bake in the oven for 35 to 40 minutes, until fully cooked.
  4. Top meatloaf with shredded mozzarella cheese.
